# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Moataz Zaky Portfolio

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** Moataz Zaky - The Integrated Operator  
**Tagline:** "Turning a big dream into a big reality"  
**Target Audience:** Recruiters, Partners, and Potential Clients in the Operations, Full-Stack Development, and Cybersecurity sectors.

## 2. Core Objectives
- Showcase professional journey as an "Integrated Operator."
- Highlight the intersection of Business Operations and Technical Implementation.
- Provide a centralized hub for all projects, certifications, and contact information.
- Enable bilingual accessibility (Arabic/English) for local and international markets.

## 3. UI/UX Design Strategy
### Visual Identity
- **Primary Style:** Dark, futuristic "Cyber-Ops" theme.
- **Color Palette:** Deep navy/black backgrounds with neon blue, green, and purple accents.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Inter/JetBrains Mono) for readability and a technical feel.

### Interactive Elements
- **Mode Toggle:** "Business" vs. "Tech" switch to filter content relevance.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile devices.
- **Animations:** Smooth scrolling, hover effects on cards, and transition animations for mode switching.
- **Language Switch:** Arabic/English toggle (Upcoming implementation).
