# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Moataz Zaky Portfolio

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** Moataz Zaky - The Integrated Operator  
**Tagline:** "Turning a big dream into a big reality"  
**Target Audience:** Recruiters, Partners, and Potential Clients in the Operations, Full-Stack Development, and Cybersecurity sectors.

## 2. Core Objectives
- Showcase professional journey as an "Integrated Operator."
- Highlight the intersection of Business Operations and Technical Implementation.
- Provide a centralized hub for all projects, certifications, and contact information.
- Enable bilingual accessibility (Arabic/English) for local and international markets.

## 3. UI/UX Design Strategy
### Visual Identity
- **Primary Style:** Dark, futuristic "Cyber-Ops" theme.
- **Color Palette:** Deep navy/black backgrounds with neon blue, green, and purple accents.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Inter/JetBrains Mono) for readability and a technical feel.

### Interactive Elements
- **Mode Toggle:** "Business" vs. "Tech" switch to filter content relevance.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile devices.
- **Animations:** Smooth scrolling, hover effects on cards, and transition animations for mode switching.

## 4. Features & Content Sections
### Hero Section
- Dynamic title and subtitle.
- Profile photo with "ACTIVE" status indicator.
- Core stats: 3+ Years Exp, 7+ Projects, 5+ Certs, 100+ Groups managed.
- Social links (LinkedIn, GitHub).

### Operations Roadmap (Experience)
- **Senior Operations Specialist @ Instant Software Solutions:** KPIs (100% Automation, 95% Error Reduction, 80% Time Saved).
- **Project Manager @ QUALIFIDERS:** Coordination and roadmap management.
- **Sales Manager @ Barah Co-working Space:** Strategy and team leadership.
- **Customer Service @ Teleperformance:** Ranking and professionalism.

### Portfolio (Projects)
- **IOMS:** Institute Operations Management System (Node.js/SQL).
- **Zoom Automation:** Zapier/Webhook pipeline saving 2+ hours daily.
- **WhatsApp CRM Extension:** Real-time lookup sidebar (JavaScript/DOM).
- **PetTouch:** NFC-based pet management (React/Supabase/i18next).
- **WhatsApp Bulk Sender:** Personalized messaging tool.
- **ZAK-Hunt:** Cybersecurity reconnaissance tool (Python).
- **Software Master:** High-performance landing platform.

### Security Vault (Certifications)
- CCNA (200-301)
- Career Essentials in Cybersecurity
- CompTIA A+ Core 1
- Cyber Security Diploma (Optical Soft)

## 5. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS, Vite.
- **Backend/Integrations:** Zapier, Supabase, Google Drive/Zoom APIs.
- **Internationalization:** i18next (Arabic/English support).
- **Deployment:** Netlify.

## 6. Bilingual Support (Upcoming)
- **English (Primary):** Current implementation.
- **Arabic (Secondary):** Full RTL support for all sections, including professional titles and project descriptions.
